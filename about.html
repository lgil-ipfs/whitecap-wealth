Leigh Grant

Founder & Wealth Advisor

Whitecap Wealth is more than a trade name; it’s a commitment to the families and business owners of Vancouver. With years of experience in the British Columbia financial sector, Leigh Grant established Whitecap Wealth to provide a more personalized, holistic approach to wealth management.

Leigh specializes in complex estate planning and multi-generational wealth transfer. His philosophy is rooted in the belief that every client deserves a strategy as unique as their own journey. By simplifying the complexities of insurance, tax, and investment, Leigh empowers his clients to look toward the future with confidence.

As a licensed insurance advisor in BC, Leigh brings a rigorous focus on risk management, ensuring that "wealth" isn't just about growth, but about protection and preservation.
